Over 100 vendors set for annual Cupertino Farmers Market

The annual Cupertino Farmers Market returns on Sunday, May 11.

Posted By: Staff Writer May 11, 2018

The Cupertino Farmers Market kicks off this Sunday, May 13 at 21267 Stevens Creek Boulevard from 9 a.m. to 1 p.m.

The West Coast Farmers Market Association is hosting the ongoing event, which will occur every Sunday from May 13 to December 30.

The annual Cupertino Farmers Market includes over 100 vendors and provides fresh and organic produce, as well as gourmet foods.
